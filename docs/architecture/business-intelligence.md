## Purpose

The Business Intelligence Domain turns VentureOS operating data into executive insight, revenue intelligence, cost intelligence, portfolio intelligence, and strategic recommendations.

- Business analytics
- Revenue intelligence
- Cost intelligence
- Growth intelligence
- Thesis performance insight
- Hypothesis outcome analysis

- Reasoning output review
- Funding readiness insight

- Dossier evidence quality insight
- Forecasting
- KPI aggregation
- Strategic recommendations
- Portfolio insights
- Cross-venture comparison
- Executive reporting

## Portfolio Intelligence

Business Intelligence must compare ventures across portfolios.

Examples:

- Most profitable
- Fastest growing
- Highest ROI
- Highest execution cost
- Highest AI spend
- Highest governance risk
- Best performing workflows
- Best performing capabilities
- Best performing theses
- Best performing hypothesis patterns
- Highest concentration risk
- Strongest evidence-backed learning patterns
- Strongest Investment Readiness Scores
- Funding plan performance
- Milestone capital release outcomes

## Rule

Business Intelligence must measure business outcomes, not technical activity. Running more workflows does not mean success. Creating measurable business value is success.

Business Intelligence must support Thesis Engine, Hypothesis Engine, Portfolio Diversification, Learning Engine, Capital Allocation, Investment, and Enterprise Value evaluation.

Business Intelligence may support future investor marketplace and funding analysis only as evidence-based, compliance-gated reporting. It must not recommend guaranteed returns or authorize regulated financial activity.

Business Intelligence may provide inputs to Strategic Review Domain, Dynamic Review Council, Debate Engine, Consensus Engine, and Investment Memo Generator. It does not make final business decisions.

Business Intelligence may use Enterprise Knowledge Graph, Organizational Memory, Learning Engine, and Reasoning Engine outputs for governed analysis. It must preserve the distinction between facts, Evidence, Decisions, assumptions, forecasts, opinions, learning, and reasoning outputs.
