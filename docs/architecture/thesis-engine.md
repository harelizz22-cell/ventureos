# Thesis Engine

Parent: `docs/architecture/ventureos-architecture.md`

Source Of Truth: This file is the source of truth for Thesis Engine architecture.

## Purpose

The Thesis Engine defines the Founder and Organization investment/build thesis.

It provides the strategic filter for Ventures, external investments, acquisitions, and capital allocations before capital or execution moves forward.

## Thesis Inputs

The Thesis Engine must define:

- Preferred markets
- Excluded markets
- Preferred business models
- Geography
- Risk tolerance
- Capital range
- Expected return
- Time horizon
- Strategic constraints
- Thesis compliance scoring

## Thesis Alignment Review

Thesis alignment review must evaluate whether a proposed Venture, external investment, acquisition, or capital allocation fits the active Founder and Organization thesis.

The review must produce an auditable recommendation and identify evidence, uncertainty, exceptions, and required approval.

## Rule

No Venture, external investment, acquisition, or capital allocation may proceed without Thesis alignment review.

Thesis compliance scoring supports governed decisions. It does not override Founder approval, governance, evidence, capital discipline, or Enterprise Value evaluation.
