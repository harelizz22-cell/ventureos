# Investment Marketplace

Parent: `docs/architecture/ventureos-architecture.md`

Source Of Truth: This file is the source of truth for Investment Marketplace architecture.

## Purpose

The Investment Marketplace defines future architecture for exposing selected VentureOS opportunities to qualified investors through governed, compliance-gated, evidence-based investor-facing views.

This is not enabled in MVP and does not authorize public fundraising, securities sales, investor fund custody, or investment execution.

## Responsibilities

- Investor-facing opportunity dossiers
- Investment round visibility
- Opportunity comparison
- Risk disclosure structure
- Evidence package presentation
- Funding progress visibility
- Investor communication records
- Compliance-gated investor access

## Marketplace Boundary

The marketplace may present approved opportunity information only after Investment Readiness review, Investment Dossier preparation, Founder approval, and legal/compliance gating.

Investor-facing content must distinguish assumptions, evidence, forecasts, and confirmed facts.

## Rule

The marketplace is a future governed capability.

It must not directly sell securities or process investments unless operating through a legally approved structure.

No guaranteed return language is allowed.

## Placeholder Status

This is a future-phase architecture placeholder. It does not define investor onboarding, payment processing, securities offering mechanics, KYC/AML, broker-dealer workflows, legal entity structure, schemas, UI, integrations, or implementation.
