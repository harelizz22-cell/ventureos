# Investor Intelligence

Parent: `docs/architecture/ventureos-architecture.md`

Source Of Truth: This file is the source of truth for Investor Intelligence architecture.

## Purpose

Investor Intelligence defines future architecture for matching investors to opportunities based on investor profile, thesis alignment, suitability signals, compliance status, and opportunity fit.

This is recommendation-only unless future legal/compliance review explicitly permits additional behavior.

## Investor Profile Inputs

Investor profile may include:

- Preferred sectors
- Geography
- Risk tolerance
- Capital range
- Time horizon
- Expected return profile
- Impact interests
- Previous investments
- Excluded industries
- Compliance status

## Matching Outputs

Investor Intelligence may recommend:

- Investor-opportunity fit
- Thesis alignment
- Risk fit
- Capital range fit
- Suitability review needs
- Compliance review needs
- Exclusion or restriction flags

## Rule

Investor matching is recommendation only unless future compliance permits otherwise.

Investor Intelligence must not approve investor access, sell securities, process investments, or move money.

## Placeholder Status

This is a future-phase architecture placeholder. It does not define investor onboarding, suitability rules, regulated advice, schemas, integrations, models, or implementation.
