## Purpose

The Founder Decision Graph makes every Founder decision traceable to evidence, execution, business outcome, financial outcome, and long-term enterprise value.

## Graph Path

Decision
-> Evidence
-> Execution
-> Business Outcome
-> Financial Outcome
-> Long-term Enterprise Value

## Rule

The system must eventually explain the impact of every strategic decision.
