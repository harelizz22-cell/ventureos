# Cross-Venture Intelligence

Parent: `docs/architecture/business-intelligence.md`

Source Of Truth: This file is the source of truth for Cross-Venture Intelligence architecture.

## Purpose

Allow portfolio-wide intelligence without breaking Venture isolation.

Cross-Venture Intelligence supports governed comparison, aggregation, learning, benchmarking, and reasoning across Ventures while preserving Organization, Portfolio, Venture, role, policy, Evidence, Decision, and Audit boundaries.

## Aggregate Analytics

Aggregate analytics may summarize Venture performance across a Portfolio or Organization.

Aggregate outputs must not reveal raw Venture data unless Policy Engine explicitly allows access.

## Portfolio KPIs

Portfolio KPIs may include revenue, cost, profit, Enterprise Value contribution, execution reliability, governance risk, evidence quality, stage progression, capital efficiency, and portfolio diversification.

KPI definitions must remain explainable and auditable.

## Trend Detection

Trend detection may identify movement across Ventures, markets, costs, growth, customer behavior, risk, evidence quality, and execution reliability.

Trend outputs must identify scope, source, confidence, limitations, and freshness where meaningful.

## Pattern Recognition

Pattern recognition may identify repeated success patterns, repeated failure patterns, pricing patterns, GTM patterns, execution issues, learning patterns, and capital efficiency patterns.

Pattern recognition is advisory and does not make final Decisions.

## Governed Cross-Venture Reasoning

Reasoning across Ventures must use approved Knowledge, Organizational Memory, Evidence, and scoped data.

Reasoning Engine outputs remain recommendations only and do not become Evidence automatically.

## Evidence Aggregation

Evidence aggregation may summarize Evidence across Ventures, but must preserve Evidence quality, source, freshness, suitability, and scope.

Aggregated Evidence does not replace underlying Evidence records.

## Benchmarking

Benchmarking may compare Ventures, Capabilities, Workflows, costs, conversion, reliability, growth, risk, or Enterprise Value contribution.

Benchmarking must not expose restricted raw Venture data to unauthorized users, Agents, or Organizations.

## Shared Learning

Shared learning may transfer lessons across Ventures when policy allows.

Learning must remain evidence-backed, auditable, and subject to quarantine, dispute, or withdrawal where required.

## Rule

Raw Venture data must not leak between Ventures unless policy explicitly allows.

## Placeholder Status

This is architecture documentation only. It does not define analytics schemas, dashboards, query engines, storage, services, models, or implementation.
