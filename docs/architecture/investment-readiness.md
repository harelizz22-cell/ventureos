# Investment Readiness

Parent: `docs/architecture/ventureos-architecture.md`

Source Of Truth: This file is the source of truth for Investment Readiness architecture.

## Purpose

Investment Readiness determines whether a Venture is mature enough to be shown to investors in a future governed marketplace or funding process.

This review protects Founder control, investor-facing quality, legal/compliance boundaries, evidence integrity, and Enterprise Value discipline.

## Readiness Dimensions

Investment Readiness review must evaluate:

- Thesis alignment
- Hypothesis maturity
- Evidence quality
- Market validation
- Technical feasibility
- Business model clarity
- Budget plan
- Milestone plan
- Legal/compliance review
- Intellectual property status
- Risk profile
- Governance status
- Financial model quality

## Output

Investment Readiness Score.

## Rule

No Venture may be exposed to investors before passing Investment Readiness review.

Investment Readiness does not authorize securities transactions, fundraising activity, investor access, capital custody, or money movement without legal/compliance approval.
