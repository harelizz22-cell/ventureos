# ADR-075: Capital Stress Simulator

Status: Accepted

Date: 2026-07-01

## Context

VentureOS must anticipate adverse financial conditions before committing capital at portfolio scale.

## Decision

Adopt Capital Stress Simulator for market crash, venture failure, double budget, zero revenue, delayed launch, fundraising failure, and multiple simultaneous failure scenarios.

Stress simulation must produce recommended actions, capital preservation strategy, confidence, and remaining runway.

## Rationale

Stress testing helps preserve Enterprise Value and runway under uncertainty.

## Consequences

- Major capital decisions may require fresh stress simulation where policy requires it.
- Simulation output must identify assumptions and uncertainty.
- Simulation output does not move money and is not Evidence until promoted through governance.

## Enforcement

Policy Engine may require stress simulation before capital approval, release, reallocation, or freeze removal.
