# ADR-031: Investment Readiness

Parent: `docs/adr/README.md`

Source Of Truth: This file is the source of truth for the accepted Investment Readiness decision.

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Future investor-facing opportunities need a maturity gate before any Venture is shown to investors. Readiness must include thesis alignment, hypothesis maturity, evidence quality, validation, feasibility, budget, milestones, legal/compliance review, IP, risk, governance, and financial model quality.

## Decision

VentureOS adopts Investment Readiness as a future first-class architecture concept.

No Venture may be exposed to investors before passing Investment Readiness review.

## Alternatives Considered

- Let Founder discretion alone determine investor exposure.
- Use Opportunity Score alone as readiness.
- Delay readiness until a marketplace is implemented.

## Rationale

Opportunity quality and investor suitability require more than scoring. Investment Readiness protects evidence integrity, risk disclosure, governance, compliance, and Founder control before investor-facing exposure.

## Risks

- Readiness scoring may become misleading without clear criteria.
- Legal/compliance status may be treated as complete when only preliminary.
- Investor exposure may occur before evidence is mature.

## Compliance Notes

Investment Readiness does not authorize fundraising, securities transactions, investor access, or capital movement. Legal/compliance review remains mandatory.

## Consequences

- Investment Readiness Score becomes a future investor-facing gate.
- Investment Dossier and marketplace exposure depend on readiness.
- Business Intelligence, Opportunity Score, Investment Engine, and Enterprise Value Engine must account for readiness where meaningful.

## Enforcement

No Venture may be exposed to investors before passing Investment Readiness review and required legal/compliance gates.
