# ADR-028: Hypothesis Engine

Parent: `docs/adr/README.md`

Source Of Truth: This file is the source of truth for the accepted Hypothesis Engine decision.

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Architecture Audit #006 requires every Venture to begin as an explicit, testable business hypothesis before validation begins.

## Decision

VentureOS adopts the Hypothesis Engine as a first-class architecture concept.

No Venture may move from idea to validation without an approved hypothesis.

## Consequences

- Venture validation must start from explicit assumptions, success criteria, kill criteria, expected ROI, and Thesis alignment.
- Build activity remains blocked until validation and phase approval requirements are satisfied.
- Hypothesis quality becomes a strategic input to Opportunity Score, Business Intelligence, Capital Allocation, and Enterprise Value evaluation.

## Enforcement

Hypothesis Engine governs idea-to-validation readiness. It does not authorize production build, capital movement, external integration, or autonomous execution.
