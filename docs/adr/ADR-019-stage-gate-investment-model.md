# ADR-019: Stage Gate Investment Model

Parent: `docs/adr/README.md`

Source Of Truth: This file is the source of truth for the accepted Stage Gate Investment Model decision.

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Venture investment risk changes across lifecycle stages. Investment decisions must be gated by evidence, risk, governance, expected ROI, and capital recommendation.

## Decision

VentureOS adopts mandatory stage gates for Venture investment progression.

Venture lifecycle stages are Idea, Validation, Prototype, MVP, Revenue, Growth, Scale, Mature, Exit, and Archive.

## Consequences

- Investment rules differ at every stage.
- Stage transitions require evidence, expected ROI, risk score, governance approval, and capital recommendation.
- Founder-defined maximum investment applies at Stage 0.

## Enforcement

No stage transition may bypass evidence, risk scoring, governance approval, and capital recommendation requirements.
