# ADR-025: Thesis Engine

Parent: `docs/adr/README.md`

Source Of Truth: This file is the source of truth for the accepted Thesis Engine decision.

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Architecture Audit #006 adds strategic thinking engines required before Phase 2. VentureOS needs an explicit Founder and Organization thesis so Ventures, external investments, acquisitions, and capital allocations are evaluated against strategic intent before execution or capital recommendation.

## Decision

VentureOS adopts the Thesis Engine as a first-class architecture concept.

No Venture, external investment, acquisition, or capital allocation may proceed without Thesis alignment review.

## Consequences

- Thesis alignment becomes a required strategic gate.
- Thesis compliance scoring becomes an input to Opportunity Score, Capital Allocation, Investment, and Enterprise Value evaluation.
- Exceptions require evidence, governance, and approval.

## Enforcement

Thesis Engine generates governed strategic review and compliance scoring. It does not execute investments, move money, or override Founder approval.
