# ADR-034: Milestone-Based Capital Release

Parent: `docs/adr/README.md`

Source Of Truth: This file is the source of truth for the accepted Milestone-Based Capital Release decision.

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Future funding should avoid releasing large amounts of capital all at once. Capital release needs milestone evidence, governance, legal/compliance boundaries, and auditability.

## Decision

VentureOS adopts Milestone Capital Release as a future first-class architecture concept.

Capital release is divided into milestones. Every release requires evidence and governance review.

## Alternatives Considered

- Release all capital at round close.
- Treat release timing as manual notes only.
- Let execution workflows release capital without milestone evidence.

## Rationale

Milestone-based release reduces waste, improves evidence discipline, supports Founder control, and links capital use to measurable progress.

## Risks

- Milestone criteria may be vague.
- Failed milestones may create investor communication or legal obligations.
- Unused capital handling may be undefined without legal/compliance structure.

## Compliance Notes

Unused capital handling must be defined by legal/compliance structure.

Milestone Capital Release does not authorize VentureOS to hold funds, transfer funds, execute securities workflows, or move money autonomously.

## Consequences

- Funding plans must include milestone success criteria.
- Capital release recommendations require evidence.
- Failed milestones may pause or stop Ventures.

## Enforcement

Milestone Capital Release may recommend release, pause, stop, or review actions. It must not move money or execute regulated financial activity.
