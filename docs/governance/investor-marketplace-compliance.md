# Investor Marketplace Compliance

Parent: `docs/governance/governance-rules.md`

Source Of Truth: This file is the source of truth for future investor marketplace compliance boundaries.

## Purpose

Define the compliance boundary for any future VentureOS investor marketplace, funding process, investor-facing dossier, investor matching, syndicated funding model, or milestone-based capital release architecture.

## Compliance Boundary

- VentureOS must not directly hold investor funds unless legally permitted.
- VentureOS must not execute securities transactions without appropriate licensing or approved legal structure.
- Jurisdiction-specific legal review is mandatory.
- Investor access may need qualification checks.
- Public fundraising may trigger securities regulation.
- Medical, biotech, financial, and regulated opportunities require enhanced review.
- All investment content must distinguish evidence from forecast.
- No guaranteed return language is allowed.

## Rule

Future investor marketplace and funding capabilities are governance-gated and compliance-gated.

No regulated financial activity is approved by documentation alone.

No autonomous money movement is allowed.

This is a future-phase governance placeholder. It does not define legal advice, licensing strategy, securities offering documents, KYC/AML process, payment processing, integrations, or implementation.
