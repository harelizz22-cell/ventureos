# Component Rules

Parent: `docs/design/README.md`

Source Of Truth: This file is the source of truth for high-level VentureOS component design rules.

## Founder Status Bar

Shows current founder attention requirements, governance state, operating posture, and urgent system status. It should be persistent, restrained, and immediately readable.

## Decision Card

Represents a decision requiring review, approval, deferral, or rejection. It must show decision owner, evidence status, risk status, approval requirement, and next action.

## Venture Card

Represents a venture or opportunity. It must prioritize status, validation stage, evidence quality, risk, owner, and next milestone.

## Evidence Card

Represents a piece or bundle of evidence. It must show source, confidence, timestamp, quality, and relationship to a decision or opportunity.

## Risk Card

Represents a material risk. It must show severity, category, mitigation, review owner, and blocking status.

## Audit Timeline

Shows meaningful events, approvals, state changes, and decisions in chronological order. It must be factual, traceable, and easy to scan.

## Tables

Tables are preferred for dense operational comparison. They must support clear state, ownership, timestamps, and action visibility.

## Side Navigation

Side navigation should expose primary operating areas without decorative naming. It should remain stable and predictable.

## Command Actions

Command actions should be explicit, permission-aware, and clearly tied to the current object or workflow.

## Approval Dialogs

Approval dialogs must show decision summary, evidence, risk, policy implications, and consequences before approval.

## Empty States

Empty states should be direct and operational. They should explain what is absent and the next valid action.

## Loading States

Loading states should preserve layout stability and avoid theatrical animation.

## Error States

Error states must distinguish failure, missing permission, governance block, and unavailable data.
